HR Manager of India
Location: Bidadi, Bangalore Report Line: Group HR Manager Package Salary: 16 Lacs

Job Description:

1. End to End recruitment Process;
2. Employee Connect and Employee Development;
3. Taken care of employee benefits;
4. Implementation of Training Programs;
5. Establish the Related Policy;
6. Deal with the Labor department for HR Issue and certificate and registration of Labor;
7. Handle the Blue collar salary.

Requirement:

1. Bachelor Degree or above;
2. Minimum 8 years HR Management experience especially in Manufacture Industry;
3. Fluent in Oral English and Kanataka and Tamil;
4. Have strong relation of Bidadi Area’s Government and Political leaders;
5. Have the resource of Blue collar workers especially sewing worker in bag industry or Garment.
Factory Manager of India
Location: Bidadi, Bangalore Report Line: Operation Director of India Package Salary: 12 Lacs

Job Description:

1. Leading the permanent & contractual workforce of 300 employees;
2. Developing processes to enhance sales & order acquisition;
3. Supervising daily capacity;
4. Managing overall operations for increasing production on the shop-floor within cost & time norms;
5. Creating budgets, wastage norms and driving strict adherence of final products to set quality parameters;
6. Administering QA department for inspection & taking corrective action in the event of non-conformities;
7. Meeting the standard of international.

Requirement:

1. Diploma Degree or above;
2. Minimum 10 years factory management experience especially in Garment or Bag industry;
3. Fluent in Oral English and Kanataka and Tamil;
4. Have strong relation of Local political leaders;
5. Have the resource of Blue collar workers especially sewing worker in bag industry or Garment.
